Hello! My name is Benjamin! A little about me: For over a decade I spent my life in sales and then switched to working as a fashion stylist in eCommerce photos studios. After 6 years of working freelance, I settled down and got married to my awesome wife Morgan. We currently have 2 kids and a 3rd on the way. I have been a stay at home dad for the last 4 years, but am currently taking steps to re-enter the workforce. At present I'm taking a full-stack web development bootcamp with UNCC. My hope is to use the knowledge and skills I gain from class to land a job in the web development world. Thank you for visiting my mock portfolio page!
